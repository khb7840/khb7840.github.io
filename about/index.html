About

Hyunbin Kim is a PhD student in SteineggerLab in Seoul National University, South Korea. His expertise includes bioinformatics, cancer genomics, protein structure analysis, data visualization, and tool development. He is the developer and maintainer of FIREVAT, Foldcomp and Folddisco.
